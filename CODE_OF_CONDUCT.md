# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically constructive project community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Discuss ideas and evidence rather than people.
- Welcome contributors with different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Protect private device, application, and network data.
- Accept maintainers' safety and scope decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disclosure of private information, abusive language, and misuse of the project against systems without authorization are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers through the repository host. Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or reject contributions to protect the community and project users.
